The Story

Builder.
Disruptor.
Servant Leader.

Ryan Harrell is a Fayetteville, NC-based entrepreneur with over two decades of experience building brands, leading companies, and creating ecosystems that others said couldn't exist.

As the co-founder of American Tattoo Society, Ryan turned a single tattoo studio near Fort Bragg into the most recognized tattoo brand serving military communities across the United States — the pioneers who wrote the regulations for on-base tattooing with the U.S. Army.

As the founder of the All American Tattoo Convention, he created one of the most respected tattoo events on the East Coast, raising over $100,000 for veteran-focused nonprofits since 2016.

Before ATS, Ryan built and sold three award-winning marketing firms, earning recognition as one of 40 Under 40 in Southeastern NC and winning 60+ national and international awards for design, marketing, and business excellence.

His mission is clear: keep the tattoo industry controlled by tattooing, keep military communities served, and keep building what others only talk about.

Military Community Roots

Born and raised in Fayetteville — home of Fort Bragg and the 82nd Airborne. Ryan's connection to military culture isn't a marketing angle; it's personal.

Digital Marketing Pioneer

Co-founder of ARC Design Agency and multiple marketing firms. Ryan has been building brands online since before social media existed.

40 Under 40 Honoree

Named one of the top young entrepreneurs in Southeastern North Carolina. Advisor to both FTCC (Fayetteville Technical Community College) and FSU (Fayetteville State University) — contributing expertise to entrepreneurship and creative programs in his hometown.

Philanthropy in Action

Board member at Operation Tattooing Freedom — providing therapeutic tattooing for veterans managing combat-related PTSD and trauma.
